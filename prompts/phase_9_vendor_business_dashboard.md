# Phase 9 — Salon Business Control Dashboard

Goal:
Create a professional dashboard for salon owners.

Dashboard sections:
1. Today
- appointments today
- open slots
- staff working
- expected revenue
- low stock alerts

2. Revenue
- daily revenue
- weekly revenue
- monthly revenue
- revenue by service
- revenue by technician

3. Inventory
- low stock
- restock cost
- usage trend
- supplier savings

4. Customers
- repeat customers
- inactive customers
- top customers
- missed follow-up opportunities

5. AI Suggestions
- price changes
- restock recommendations
- marketing ideas
- staffing suggestions

Rules:
- Mobile friendly
- Vendor-specific
- No cross-vendor data leaks
- Must not slow down customer-facing pages

Acceptance tests:
- Dashboard loads for vendor
- Data belongs only to that vendor
- Low stock appears
- Revenue summaries appear
- AI suggestions appear
